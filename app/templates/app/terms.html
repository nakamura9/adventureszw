Booking Conditions

Tour bookings can only be done online and should be made as early as possible to guarantee the desired tour and itinerary. Joining a Tour under the influence of drugs, medicines and/or excessive alcohol is not permitted.

Change of Booking

Changes in bookings (i.e. any change in the tour, the number of Adventurers or in the tour date) must be made in writing (e-mail) and are legally binding upon confirmation by Adventures ZW. For any change of booking a handling fee of US$5 per person in addition to any additional costs of the tour is due. Any change of date or tour cannot be guaranteed and shall be applied subject to availability.

Last minute bookings

For bookings made less than 7 days prior to the tour, we charge the full amount. No refund in case of cancellation.

Rates and Terms of Payment

All tour prices are quoted per person (unless otherwise stated) in US dollars. Prices are subject to change according to the section below. A non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking, balance will be paid for in cash prior to the selected tour. Please refer to Our Adventures to see a detailed description of what is included in your tour price.

Adventures ZW reserves the right to change the published tour prices and currency at any given moment. Changes in prices in case of extraordinary circumstances (3rd party rate change) will be communicated in writing within 3 working days of being notified. Any additional costs of the tour shall be borne by the Adventurer. Adventures ZW undertakes to immediately inform the Adventurer of any changes in services and/or in the program. Complaints

If you have any complaints while on tour, we advise you to address your tour guide and supplier of service right at the spot and we will do our utmost to find the best possible solution to your problem.

Please feel free to send us a detailed complaint within 20 days after a contractually agreed tour in writing to admin@adventures.co.zw. Include details such as, what caused your dissatisfaction, the exact date of tour and tour guide, as well as solutions given whilst on tour. Travel Insurance

Adventurer’s need to keep in mind that safaris, adventure travel and particularly any physical activity like trekking long distances or climbing involve a certain risk factor and inherent dangers. Adventures ZW reserves the right to accept or retain participation for any person on a tour at any given time if we consider someone unsuitable for the trip. In this case, we will refund a fair and reasonable amount. ​

Adventures ZW must be informed in writing of any physical disability and food allergies requiring attention or treatment at the time of the reservation. We strongly advise you to arrange adequate insurance against any possible risk while on tour. The travel insurance should cover medical expenses and repatriation, personal accidents, tour cancellation and any kind of loss.

Transportation

The transportation provided for you will be according to your route and number of participants. English speaking drivers and/or guides are included in your transportation and tour. Adequate health and accident insurance coverage is the responsibility of the Adventurer (including supplementary insurance against sports accidents in case of Tours with sports activities).

Furthermore, Adventurers are obliged to wear seat belts and to use child safety seats, if applicable. Adventures ZW reserves the right to exclude an Adventurer from the tour with immediate effect in case of non-compliance with the terms of participation or violation of any instructions. In case a minor Adventurer is excluded, the accompanying person is also excluded from the Tour. In case the exclusion is made prior to the tour start, the terms of cancellation apply analogously. In case the exclusion is made during a tour, the Adventurer has no right to a refund of the tour price. In case of exclusion, any and all claims for damages against Adventures ZW are excluded.

If the Adventurer is not present in time at the departure respectively meeting point for the start of or during the tour, Adventures ZW reserves the right to exclude the Adventurer from the tour after a waiting period of 10 minutes and to depart without the Adventurer. In this case, the Adventurer is neither entitled to a refund of the tour price nor may the Adventurer claim any damages.
